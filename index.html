About AG Mortgage Bank Plc

AG Mortgage Bank Plc was founded in 2004 with the Assemblies of God Nigeria as the core investor (51.9% shareholding). Our objective is to support low and medium income families to own affordable and livable homes through the provision of unique mortgage services. Operating as a CBN-Licensed National Mortgage Bank since January 2005, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner in Nigeria's housing finance sector.

Our Mission

To provide accessible and affordable mortgage financing solutions that empower Nigerians to achieve home ownership while maintaining the highest standards of service excellence and ethical business practices.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's No. 1 Housing Finance Group by 2030, recognized for innovation, sustainability, and positive social impact in the Nigerian housing sector.

Core Values

Faith-based business principles, integrity, customer-centricity, innovation, sustainability, and commitment to empowering Nigerian families through affordable housing finance.
